    Shape Selection Pane
    In This Topic

    The shape selection pane is a side panel used to manage and arrange multiple shapes in a worksheet. You can access this panel through the Design tab for shapes.

    Select Shapes

    You can select shapes in the worksheet by clicking their corresponding names in the shape selection pane. The selected name's background color will change to green to indicate the selection.

    Multiple shapes can be selected by holding Ctrl and clicking on the shape name.

    Rename Shapes

    You can double-click a shape's name to rename the shape. Press the Enter key or click outside the pane to confirm the rename.

    Show or Hide Shapes

    The button beside each shape name controls the visibility of the shape. The circular button indicates that the shape is currently visible in the worksheet.

    You can hide the specified shape by clicking the button. The hyphen (en dash) button indicates that the shape is hidden.

    The visibility setting of all the shapes in a group is disabled if a group shape is hidden.

    You can also show or hide all the shapes in the worksheet by selecting the "Show All" or "Hide All" options in the selection pane.

    Set zIndex of Shapes

    You can arrange the shapes according to their zIndex in the worksheet. Manage the stack order of the shapes by selecting the shape name and clicking the up/down buttons in the selection pane. Alternatively, you can click and drag the shape name in the selection pane list to arrange the zIndex.

    The zIndex of individual shapes in a group shape can only set by dragging operation.
